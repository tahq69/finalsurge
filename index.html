  • Daily Workout View

    The Daily View shows basic workout data, plus whether the workout is planned or completed. The workout is color-coded to indicate whether the workout was completed as planned (Green)… and if not completed as planned, color-coding indicates whether the workout was simply missed (Red)… or was under, or over, performed (Yellow).

  • Weekly Workout View

    The Weekly View doesn’t require you to awkwardly put your phone into a landscape position. An entire week of workouts is shown and workouts are color-coded to instantly show you the status of each workout. The Weekly View also shows planned and completed weekly totals by workout type to confirm if you are on plan with just a glance.

  • Easily Enter Workouts

    The Complete As Planned button on the workout entry screen automatically enters Distance, Duration, and Pace, plus you can easily add How You Felt, your Perceived Effort, and Workout Notes. The App automatically syncs from your tracking devices through platforms like Garmin Connect®, Strava®, MapMyRun®, and others.

  • Splits, Maps, Graphs & More

    If you are using a sports watch, bike computer or similar tracking device and want to dive into every detail about this workout, the App shows laps (splits), the workout map and route, selectable workout graphs, charts, and HR and power zones, gear, weather, and more.

  • Coach Athlete Communication

    Easy and effective Coach and Athlete communication is one of the most important tools in accomplishing fitness and performance success. The App uses the familiar text messaging conversation format to support and recall conversations for each and every workout as needed.

  • Coach or Athlete or Both

    Unlike others, with Final Surge App you just log in once if you are both a Coach and an Athlete. Coaches can easily review and manage their Athletes’ and Teams’ Workout Calendars. Team and Group calendars, internal message boards and individual messaging make this App ideal for high school, collegiate, professional and club teams.
